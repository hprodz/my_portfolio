About Sweet Solace

Sweet Solace is an affordable product containing mini coconut macaroons and an ordinary earphone wrapped individually and tied together that is budget friendly for every student’s everyday allowance. This product aims to provide relaxation for students during their studies by lessening students’ frazzle due to stress in school activities. With the coconut macaroon to suppress hypothalamic pituitary adrenal (HPA) to axis in their brain, an earphone canrelease endorphins and improve the students’ senses of well-being distractingthem and reducing physical and emotional stress levels. Sweet Solace will bemarketed in face-to-face interaction within Bukidnon State University and through an online medium marketing.
